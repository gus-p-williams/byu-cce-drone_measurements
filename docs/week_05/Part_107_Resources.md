# Week 5 — Part 107 Study Resources

A curated set of study resources for the FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot test, organized by resource type with brief descriptions and recommended use.

## Key Takeaways
- **Official Sources:** Use FAA DroneZone and LAANC for real-world operations after certification.
- **Study Strategy:** Combine full-length reviews with targeted topic drills for weak areas.
- **Practice:** Use both Rupprecht and King Schools practice exams to simulate test pacing.

---

## Official FAA Resources
- [FAA Part 107 (regulations)](https://www.faa.gov/uas/commercial_operators/part_107/){target='_blank'} — Official rule text and authoritative guidance.
- [LAANC (airspace authorization)](https://www.faa.gov/uas/research_development/airspace_integration/laanc/){target='_blank'} — Overview of near-real-time controlled-airspace authorizations.
- [FAA DroneZone](https://faadronezone.faa.gov/){target='_blank'} — Portal for registration, waivers, and account management.
- [AviationWeather.gov](https://www.aviationweather.gov/){target='_blank'} — Centralized aviation weather products (METARs, TAFs).
- [FAA NOTAMs / TFRs](https://notams.aim.faa.gov/notamSearch/){target='_blank'} — Searchable NOTAM and Temporary Flight Restriction data.

## Recommended Study Strategy
1. **Initial Pass:** Watch the [Rapid Slide Overview](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO1CXPsCKoQ) to map out the domain.
2. **Deep Dive:** Watch the [Matt Kendall Full Review](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB9qzXaQ72s) and note timestamps for topics that seem confusing.
3. **Targeted Drills:** Use the topic-focused playlists to master weak areas.
4. **Simulated Testing:** Alternate between Rupprecht and King Schools practice tests to validate readiness and identify persistent errors.
5. **Final Review:** Return to the [Knowledge Review](Part_107_Knowledge_Review.md) page for a final check of mnemonics and key facts.
